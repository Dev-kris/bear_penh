Welcome to The Bear Penh, the media company behind breakout shows such as Vampire: Blood on the Bayou and Thala Unbound as seen on Twitch and YouTube.


Founded in 2021, we have produced a growing number of recurring series, miniseries, and one-shot productions of all sorts. Our team is dedicated to producing shows and media for nerds, by nerds.

We strive to deliver top quality entertaining content, and provide a diverse and secure place for our community to explore their favorite hobbies. Whether you're into tabletop role playing games, podcasting, video games, astrology, language exchange, art, or audiobooks you've got a place by the Honey Pot.
