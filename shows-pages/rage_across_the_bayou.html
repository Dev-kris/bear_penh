Gaia is dying.

For centuries, Garou have fought the forces that threaten her existence. But they are losing.

Fueled by loss, driven by unquenchable desire to find some way forward, one pack seeks to find alliances in the most unlikely of places.

Rage Across The Bayou is a Werewolf: The Apocalypse 20th Anniversary Edition live play, set in the city of New Orleans. Join our pack as they negotiate a fragile and new ideal of Supernatural Coexistence, while dealing with the forces of The Wyrm and entrenched Garou mindsets.

Meet the cast...

The Werewolf Den: Storytellers

Played by Amylin & Ryan

This two-headed giant birthed all of creation, good and ill. We provide kindling for stifled hope and a glimmer of light in the foulest pit for all those who rage against the despair of Gaia. However, as with the Triat, our offerings are unwell and unbalanced. For those without the stomach to endure it, we do offer free dance instruction.

Finn Aegus

Played by Ares

For years Finn has had only one thing on his mind, the complete and utter destruction of every Pentex owned company, organization and shell-company. His philosophy was simple, if they make technology, he’ll make it better and cleaner. If they find some new way to poison the Earth, he’ll come up with 5 more ways to heal it. He would make whatever deals he needed to ensure that they suffer with each and every breath he takes… but then, he became a father. Now, he finds himself navigating new waters in trying to achieve his ambitions and not to let the hatred that has poisoned him for so long infect a new generation of Garou. But, will the rest of the nation accept his ideas? Or, will he find himself branded and hunted… only time will tell.

Saint

Played by Cy

Saint finds her home in the local wrestling scene of whatever town she happens to find herself in. She splits her time between running her wrestling company, taking care of her daughter, and fighting for Gaia. She cares deeply for her kinfolk and tries to give anyone she comes across who needs one a home within her little family.

Cierva de Todas las Adas

played by Velvet Divine

Child of the deep wood, born claw first and chill none. Taboo made triumph and breaker of bondage. Thunder and lightning trapped inside wolf-carved oak, fighting for Gaia and to carve faer own place on it. Cierva was named stag among faeries but remembered a lion among wolves.
